# Privacy Policy

**The Golden Mic — Pune**
Last updated: June 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

The Golden Mic ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding it when you use our website thegoldenmic.in.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
When making a booking, we collect:
- Full name
- Gender and age
- Phone number
- Email address
- Number of people in your group
- Singer idol / music preference
- Booking preferences (date, time, studio, session type)
- Payment transaction ID or screenshot

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Google Account information** (name, email, profile picture) if you choose to sign in with Google — used only for authentication
- Basic usage data via Vercel (page visits, browser type) — no personally identifiable information

### 2.3 Payment Information
- We do **not** store your card number, CVV, UPI PIN, or any sensitive payment credentials.
- All payment processing is handled by **Razorpay**, which is PCI-DSS compliant. Their privacy policy is available at razorpay.com.
- We only store the transaction reference ID for booking confirmation purposes.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---|---|
| Processing and confirming your booking | Name, phone, email, booking details |
| Sending booking confirmation and updates | Email, phone (SMS) |
| Customer support and communication | Name, phone, email |
| Preventing fraud and duplicate bookings | Phone, email, transaction ID |
| Improving our services | Aggregated, anonymised usage data |

We do **not** use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside of what is necessary to operate our service.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share your data only with:
- **Razorpay** — for payment processing
- **Supabase** — our database and authentication provider (servers located in Singapore/US)
- **Resend** — for transactional email delivery (booking confirmations)
- **Fast2SMS** — for SMS notifications (Indian provider)

All third-party providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

---

## 5. Data Storage & Security

- Your data is stored securely on **Supabase** servers.
- We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.
- Access to your booking data is restricted to authorised studio staff only.
- Payment screenshots uploaded during booking are stored in a private, access-controlled storage bucket.

---

## 6. Data Retention

- Booking records are retained for **2 years** for accounting and legal compliance purposes.
- If you request deletion of your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Request deletion** of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Opt out** of SMS/email communications (contact us directly)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at **thegoldenmicpune@gmail.com**.

---

## 8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:
- **Authentication cookies** — to keep you logged in (via Supabase Auth)
- No advertising or tracking cookies are used

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
